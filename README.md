## 🚀 How to Use

### Installation

1. Clone the repository or navigate to the project directory

2. Install the requirements

   ```bash
   pip install -r requirements.txt
   ```

3. Run the app

   ```bash
   streamlit run streamlit_app.py
   ```

4. Visit `http://localhost:8501` in your browser

### Customization Guide

Edit `streamlit_app.py` to personalize:

- **Personal Information:** Update name, email, phone, school, and location
- **Content:** Modify About section, teaching philosophy, skills, and experience
- **Metrics:** Change student count, years of experience, and success rates
- **Projects:** Add your curriculum units, research, and workshops
- **Colors:** Modify the gradient colors in the CSS styling section
- **Images:** Replace placeholder image URL with your professional photo
- **Social Links:** Add LinkedIn, Twitter, TeachersPayTeachers, and other profiles

## 🎨 Customization Highlights

### Update Your Teaching Profile
Find and replace in `streamlit_app.py`:
- `your.email@example.com` → Your email
- `+1 (XXX) XXX-XXXX` → Your phone
- `Your City, Country` → Your location
- `Your School Name` → Your school/district
- `your.email@example.com` (Contact section) → Your email

### Modify Educational Metrics
Update these values in the Home page section:
- `150+ Students Taught` → Your actual number
- `8+ Years in Education` → Your experience
- `95% Student Success Rate` → Your rate

### Add Your Curriculum Projects
Replace the sample projects with:
- Curriculum units you've designed
- PBL (Project-Based Learning) units
- Digital citizenship programs
- Interdisciplinary collaborations
- Student achievement initiatives

### Include Your Teaching Experience
Update the Experience section with:
- Your school names and locations
- Dates you taught at each
- Grade levels and subjects
- Key accomplishments and metrics
